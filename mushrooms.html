Mushrooms

I make tiny mushrooms out of polimer clay in my spare time. They are basically tiny houses, about 2cm wide and 4 to 2cm tall.

Where can you see them?

You might come across a few of my mushroom sculptures around the Belgrade city center. Their locations aren't marked, so spotting them is purely a matter of chance.

Future plans

I am very behind on making more of these. Some purple ones are due, with sparkles, really large ones as well.
