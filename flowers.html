Flowers

My second hobby is painting flowers, almost exclusively on walls.

Where can you see them?

There is a cafe in Belgrade with about 9 meter long wall covered with my flowers. It can't be missed. If you happen to find yourself in that exact cafe, you will see it for sure.

Future plans

It's always the same plan. I will paint a few more flowers and that is it. No more.


At the cafe, I mentioned above there is also an Ivy I painted, it doesn't have a single flower. That is how I envisioned it at first but after some time it started to look plain, so that Ivy needs a lot of flowers since it is about 4meters tall and 3 meters wide.
